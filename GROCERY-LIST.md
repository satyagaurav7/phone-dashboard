# Satya's Grocery List & Meal Plan

**Target: ~$80/week ($350/month) · diabetic-friendly · high-protein · Indian home-style · minimal cooking**
Built to kill the Uber Eats habit. One big shop every Saturday (that's your protocol day).

---

## THE WEEKLY SHOP (~$75–85)

### Protein (the priority — muscle + stable blood sugar)
- [ ] Chicken thighs/breast — 2 kg (~$18) · Costco or No Frills
- [ ] Eggs — 30 pack (~$7)
- [ ] Paneer — 1 block (~$6)
- [ ] Greek yogurt — large tub, plain 2% (~$6)
- [ ] Canned chickpeas / rajma — 4 tins (~$5)
- [ ] Toor dal / moong dal — 1 kg bag (~$4, lasts weeks)

### Veg (fiber = slower sugar spikes)
- [ ] Onions, tomatoes, garlic, ginger (curry base) (~$7)
- [ ] Spinach / methi — frozen bag is fine & cheap (~$3)
- [ ] Frozen mixed veg — 1 kg (~$4)
- [ ] Cucumber + 1 salad bag (~$4)
- [ ] Green chillies + coriander (~$2)

### Carbs (portion-controlled — the diabetic lever)
- [ ] Basmati rice — 5 kg lasts a month (~$12, buy monthly not weekly)
- [ ] Roti/atta — 5 kg bag (~$10, monthly)
- [ ] Rolled oats — breakfast base (~$4)

### Staples (buy ~monthly, not weekly)
- [ ] Cooking oil, salt, turmeric, chilli, garam masala, cumin
- [ ] Peanut butter (protein snack) (~$5)
- [ ] Milk (~$5/week)

**Skip / avoid:** sugary drinks, juice, white bread, packaged snacks, energy drinks — all spike blood sugar and waste money.

---

## THE MEAL PLAN (repeatable, low-effort)

**Breakfast (5 min):** Oats + Greek yogurt + peanut butter, OR 3-egg bhurji. Steady morning sugar, no crash.

**Lunch (WFH — cook fresh, this is your delivery-trigger hour):**
- Dal + rice (portion: 1 cup rice max) + salad
- OR chicken curry + 2 roti
- OR chickpea/rajma masala + rice

**Lunch (office days — prep Sunday, carry it):** Same, packed in a container. This is the single most important habit for both budget AND blood sugar.

**Dinner (light — better overnight glucose):**
- Paneer bhurji + 1 roti
- OR grilled chicken + sautéed veg (low carb)
- OR moong dal khichdi

**Post-gym:** Greek yogurt + banana, OR 2 boiled eggs, OR a scoop of paneer. Real protein beats a $20 delivery every time.

---

## SUNDAY MEAL PREP (30 min, your protocol slot)
1. Boil a dozen eggs → grab-and-go protein all week
2. Cook a big pot of dal → 3–4 lunches
3. Marinate + portion chicken into 4 bags
4. Pack Mon + Tue office lunches into containers

**One Sunday hour = zero weekday Uber Eats temptation. That's ~$400/month back in your pocket.**
