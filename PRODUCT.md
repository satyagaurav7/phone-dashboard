# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Satya — a single user. This is a private, personal daily check-in dashboard, added to the phone home screen and also opened on a laptop. Context: checking in throughout the day to log habits, track PR (permanent residence) countdowns, money numbers, and open action items. High personal stakes, self-accountability tool.

## Product Purpose

A one-person "mission control" for staying on track toward a 2027 PR goal while staying debt-free, healthy, and honest. It surfaces the numbers that matter (countdowns, credit, emergency fund), enforces daily non-negotiable habits with a streak, and holds a short list of real PR action items until each is done. Success = opening it makes the next right action obvious and keeps momentum.

## Brand Personality

Bold, motivating, coach-like. Three words: **driven, honest, relentless.** It should feel like a strong personal coach in your corner — energizing, never nagging. Voice is direct and encouraging, celebrates streaks, and never fakes progress ("my real record is enough").

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS-cream dashboards with identical hero-metric cards.
- Corporate finance-app coldness (navy + gray, lifeless).
- Overstimulating, gamified apps with confetti and noise everywhere.
- Flat gray "just data" admin panels with no emotional pull.

## Design Principles

- **Momentum first**: the streak, today's habits, and the nearest deadline are the emotional core — they lead the eye.
- **Honest by design**: real numbers, real record, no vanity metrics or fake fills.
- **Consequences are real**: habits can be stamped DONE or DENIED; a denied core habit refuses the whole day and breaks the streak — no quiet skips.
- **One decisive glance**: on a phone, the most urgent thing is obvious in under 2 seconds.
- **Earned energy**: bold color and glow reward progress; calm restraint everywhere else so it never feels loud for its own sake.
- **Tap to act**: every tile and check is a direct, obvious action — the tool disappears into the task.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Light "passport paper" theme (navy passport-cover hero, guilloche page background, stamp-styled controls) with WCAG AA contrast (body ≥4.5:1, large text ≥3:1).
- Full `prefers-reduced-motion` support (glows/animations reduce to instant/crossfade).
- Large tap targets for phone use (≥44px).
- Do not rely on color alone for state — pair with icon/label/text.
